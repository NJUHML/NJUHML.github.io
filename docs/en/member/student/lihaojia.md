Haojia Li is currently with East China Survey and Design Institute and participates in a joint postdoctoral training program with Nanjing University. He earned his Ph.D. at Nanjing University and was also a visiting scholar at the Center for Analysis and Prediction of Storms, University of Oklahoma.

## Research Interests
Mechanisms of severe convective weather, high-resolution numerical simulation, data assimilation, and typhoon prediction.
