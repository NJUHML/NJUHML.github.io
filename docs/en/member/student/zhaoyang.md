Yang Zhao is an M.S. student at the [School of Atmospheric Sciences, Nanjing University][SAS]. He completed his undergraduate study at Nanjing University and continued into graduate study in 2024.

## Research Interests
Deep-learning-based spatiotemporal downscaling, AI weather foundation models, typhoon track forecasting, and intensity correction.

## Selected Work

- Co-author of a university-level outstanding thesis team project on interpretability in deep-learning downscaling
- First inventor of a granted patent on UNet-based PPI radar echo filtering
