# Guanlong Ma

<img align="left" src="/member/picture/maguanlong.webp" width="220px" height="320px" />

Guanlong Ma is an M.S. student at the [School of Atmospheric Sciences, Nanjing University][SAS]. He previously studied atmospheric science at Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology.

## Research Interests
AI weather foundation models, meteorological downscaling, short-range precipitation forecasting and correction, and radar echo filtering.

## Awards
- First Prize, 19th Challenge Cup Special Competition “Open Competition for Top Challenges” (2024)
- 2nd place, Earth Science Track, 3rd World Science Intelligence Competition (2025)
- Second Prize, China Graduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling (2023)
- Second Prize, Jiangsu Graduate Python Competition of the Lanqiao Cup (2024)

## Selected Work
- Principal investigator, Jiangsu Graduate Research and Practice Innovation Program on deep-learning-based spatial downscaling
- Presentations on self-supervised and statistically informed meteorological downscaling
- First inventor of a granted patent on KAN-based millimeter-wave cloud radar echo identification
