Yutao Fan is a direct-entry Ph.D. student at the [School of Atmospheric Sciences, Nanjing University][SAS], after completing his undergraduate study there in 2024.

## Research Interests
Deep-learning-based precipitation diagnosis, interpretable machine learning, and predictability.

## Awards
- First Prize, 19th Challenge Cup Special Competition “Open Competition for Top Challenges” (2024)

## Selected Work
- Co-author of a university-level outstanding thesis team project on spatiotemporal downscaling of numerical model products with deep learning and interpretability
- Co-inventor of patent applications on KAN-based millimeter-wave cloud radar echo identification and UNet-based PPI radar echo filtering
