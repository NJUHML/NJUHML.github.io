# Members

## Group Leader

- [Huiling Yuan][Professor Yuan] - Distinguished Professor of the Changjiang Scholars Program (2021), Professor and Ph.D. supervisor at the [School of Atmospheric Sciences, Nanjing University][School of Atmospheric Sciences], and Deputy Director of the Key Laboratory of Mesoscale Severe Weather of the Ministry of Education.
  - Research areas: Hydrometeorological numerical modeling, AI weather models, and deep learning methods
  - Education: Ph.D., University of California, Irvine (2005)
  - Contact: `yuanhl@nju.edu.cn`; `025-89685016`
  - Current focus:
    - Hydrometeorological ensemble forecasting and deep learning simulation
    - Generative deep learning for multiscale data assimilation and prediction
    - Deep learning methods for downscaling
  - Key National Natural Science Foundation Joint Project (2024-2027):
    - Machine learning methods for refined meteorological support services in the Yangtze River Delta region
